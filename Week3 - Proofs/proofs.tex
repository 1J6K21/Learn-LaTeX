\section*{Ex 1}
Rules of Inference deduce new true statements and conclusions from premises.

one standard rule: show premise, show how premises connect, and show how premises connect to conclusion

Modus Ponens - method of approving

Modus Tollens - method of denying

Hypothetical sylloogism - chain together two conditional statements

Disjunctive syllogism - conclude one alternative is true when you know at least one of two things must be true and one of them is false.

Critical Row - $p \to q \:is\:T\:and\:q\:is\:T$
